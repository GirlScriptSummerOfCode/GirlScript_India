Story Behind the Disrupt

GirlScript Foundation is non-for-profit organization that promotes diversity in technology and helps beginners to excel. It works by establishing ‘GirlScript Chapters’ in different cities of India and helping students to be a part of fastest growing tech community and reserves 65% of seats for women.

It started in 2017 and within an year of its establishment, GirlScript is working in 14 cities of India and taught more than 3000 girls to begin their coding journey. GirlScript started as a project has been selected by Mozilla under Open Leadership program out of 51 open-source projects worldwide.The main motto of GirlScript is not only to encourage gender equality in technical sector but also to help all the beginner in technology who are clueless at the starting phase of their education.
