Welcome To GirlScript Foundation !


GirlScript Foundation is non-for-profit organization that promotes diversity in technology and helps beginners to excel. It works by establishing GirlScript Chapters’ in different cities of India and helping students to be a part of fastest growing tech community and reserves 65% of seats for women. It started inb2017 and within an year of its establishment, GirlScript is working in 14 cities of India and taught more than 3000 girls to begin their coding journey.
