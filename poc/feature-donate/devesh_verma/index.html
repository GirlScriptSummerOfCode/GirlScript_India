Why give to the GirlScript Foundation?

Our mission:

Django Girls is a volunteer-run registered charity. We use your generous donations to teach thousands of women to build websites using Python and Django by providing resources and tools volunteer organizers all over the world.

Since July 2014, our workshops have reached 15407 women in 92 countries and our online tutorial has been read by more than 500 000 people.

We're transparent about our finances. See our Annual Report for 2015 to see how we spend donations and what impact they made.
